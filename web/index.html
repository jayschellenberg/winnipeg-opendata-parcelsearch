Survey vs Assessment parcels

Survey parcels (blue on the map) are legal lots from Land Titles — the Lot / Block / Plan on a deed.

Assessment parcels (red) are the City's tax-assessed properties — one per Roll Number / civic address.

The two layers don't always align. One assessment can cover many lots, and one lot can be split between rolls. Hover anywhere on the map to see both at once.

River Lots, Outer Two Mile, Section-Township-Range

Unusual legal descriptions go in the same fields:

  • River Lots: RL in Block, parish code (JA, CH, etc.) in Plan, river-lot number in Lot.
  • Outer Two Mile lots: OT in Block, parish code in Plan.
  • Section-Township-Range like NW 10-11-4E: section 10 in Lot, township 11 in Block, range 4E in Plan, quarter NW in Description.

Partial matches work in every field.
