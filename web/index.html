Searching for River Lots or Outer Two Mile lots?

The historic parish lots are entered in the same four fields, just with different values:

  • River Lots: put RL in the Block field and the parish code in the Plan field (e.g. JA for St. James, CH for St. Charles — or another parish).
  • Outer Two Mile lots: put OT in the Block field and the parish code in the Plan field.

The Lot field takes the river-lot number as usual. Partial matches work in every field.
